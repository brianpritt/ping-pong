Ping-Pong

Ping-Pong will count up to the number you give.

Ping will transform the list of numbers by:
  • Changing numbers divisible by 3 to "ping"
  • Changing numbers divisible by 5 to "pong"
  • And numbers divisible by 15 to "ping-pong"

Your result will appear here!
